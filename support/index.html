Support

Need help? Find answers to common questions below.

How do I connect my Apple Watch?
Open the Stride AI app on your iPhone, go to Settings > Device Connection, and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your Apple Watch.
What devices are supported?
iPhone 8 or later with iOS 15.0+ and Apple Watch Series 3 or later with watchOS 8.0+.
How does the AI coaching work?
Our AI analyzes your heart rate, pace, and form in real-time to provide personalized voice guidance during your runs.
Can I use the app without an Apple Watch?
You can use basic features on iPhone alone, but real-time AI coaching requires an Apple Watch for heart rate and motion data.
Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted and we never share your personal information with third parties.
